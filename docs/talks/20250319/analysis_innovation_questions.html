first last question
Alexander Stemke How to approach academia cross sectional with research projects?
Anne-Laure Boulesteix What could be the role of a phases classification system for methodological research as described in Heinze et al. (2024)? https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/bimj.202200222
Annesh Pal Coming from core academia, how can one apply Bayesian statistics in oncological studies? What path one can follow to create statistical innovations for detection or diagnosis of cancer? What are the interesting milestones already achieved in this direction and what are the possible directions of work for the future?
Christian Kuhl How to improve cooperation and motivate Clinicians to walk beside the standard way?
Divya Gunasekar At times when there is so much competition, how can we reduce the time to get a novel product to market sooner?
Emmanuel Zuber How to avoid the double trap of 1) falling in love with your innovation and 2) losing the passion for the adequate solution to the real problem ?
Federico Mattiello What are the catalysts that help bringing innovation to fruition?
Jacob Stærk-Østergaard Can we improve collaboration across industry and academia to push novel statistical methods into acceptance from both regulators and clinicians, and if so how?
Jenny Devenport The agenda looks very good and emphasizes getting clinicians / nonstatisticians excited. While indeed a formidable challenge in the route to change, we also experience headwinds from our own function–who perhaps struggle to separate seizing opportunities with debating enhancements. In other words, how can we do a better job as statisticians to show up as a united front so that our clinicians don’t see methods as simply a matter of opinion?
Jonas Lindeløv (1) Do you have examples of important innovations that did not reach impact? Can we draw learnings from those examples? (2) What is the effect size of following all the recommendations from this session? For example, is it an increase from 10% probability of success to 25%?
Juergen Scheuenpflug In which phase of drug development do you see the greatest need to innovate (e.g. FiH to PoC or PoC - MAA/ LCM? What are the most important barriers which prevent successful innovation and how to mitigate these?
Lada Mitchell 1. Good statistical thinking, AI and maschine learning-what is a magical formula and long term vision? 2. How to inluence faster, eg bring new meaningful stats methodoly to applicatons and use?
Liliya Chamitava How can we measure the success or impact of a statistical innovation once it has been implemented into a practice?
Linard Hoessly How do you deal with the subjectivity on methodological questions in many statistical areas?
Nina Heiss Which policy challenge would you recommend focussing on.
Pernille Juul Jørgensen Q1.1: How to balance adoptations of new statistical methods with the need for fast developed clinical programmes? Q1.2: Are there sometimes you would advise to go with an “old” method instead of trying something “new” out? Q2: How much extra time do you estimate that it takes to implement a new method for a study design? Q3: Is there a regulatory risk (e.g. longer interactions or pushback?) when using a new statistical method?
Stefan Englert What measures do you take to quantify and measure impact? Which metrics are used?
Stefan Schandelmaier Hello Panel. Thanks for the seminar! We (Malena Chiaborelli, Stefan Schandelmaier) are establishing a group for methods implementation at the University of Basel. We will initially focus on “written methods guidance” which we believe plays a key role in the innovation to implementation process. We would be very interested in ideas on how we can improve methods guidance to maximise its usefulness and impact.
Susan Edwards How do you know when a new innovation’s benefit is great enough to make up for its (likely) added complexity or at least the hurdle of its unfamiliarity?
Tu Xu The biologic innovation is of no doubt to be a must in industry environment. However, statistical innovation is often considered an optional or ‘nice-to-have’ as long as regulatory requirements are met. How could we raise the importance of statistical innovation to the business and make it as a necessary part of clinical development process?
Xiaoling Wu what is the one task statistician can do better than AI?
Xiaowei Ren Is there any application of Bayesian sample size augment in the single arm trial
